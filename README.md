### Configs

* `API_KEY` Your API_KEY from [OCR Space](https://us11.list-manage.com/subscribe?u=ce17e59f5b68a2fd3542801fd&id=252aee70a1)

* `OWNER` Your Username without '@'

* `BOT_TOKEN` Your bot token from @BotFather
